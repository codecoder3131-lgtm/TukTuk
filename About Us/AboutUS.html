Our Heritage Story

A decade of navigating the majestic streets of Udaipur with local heart and professional soul.

Founded in the heart of the Old City, Tuktuk Udaipur was born from a passion for Rajasthan’s rich history and a desire to provide travelers with an authentic experience. From the towering spires of the City Palace to the serene waters of Lake Pichola, we bring you closer to the soul of our city through seamless, safe, and hospitable transport.
